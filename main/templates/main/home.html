Grit-EQ

Grit-EQ is an audio plugin that adds saturation to the frequencies that are added or subtracted from the original signal.

Input gain
Low Shelf w/ gain, frequency, and Q factor
x4: Bell w/ gain, frequency, and Q factor
High Shelf w/ gain, frequency, and Q factor
Output gain
Grit Factor, how many times the saturation is applied

Offset - When enabled, audio levels are not directly affected by gain. Selected frequencies are only saturated.
